About Me

I’m Konrad Wroński, a Data Engineer with over 3 years of experience working at SAS Institute. I specialize in creating data-driven solutions, from building machine learning models to automating processes using cloud platforms like Azure. I’m passionate about data analysis, econometrics, and using programming languages such as Python, R, and SAS to uncover insights and drive business outcomes.

Currently pursuing my Master’s in Applied Data Science at Utrecht University, I’m deepening my knowledge in areas like text and media analytics, spatial data analysis, and human network analysis. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and apply them in real-world projects.
